Agent Registration:
This module of Agent Management System allows you to register the agents, who can provide information regarding different insurance policies available for the customers. You are required to enter the details of the agents inorder to have them registered. Only upon registration, an agent would be eligible for having an appointment with a customer.
Calculate Commission:
This module allows you to calculate commission for agents. It gives you a provision to choose between calculating commission for the entire agents available or of a single agent. If you are looking for calculating commission for only one agent, this module would ask you for the respective Agent's ID. The commission is calculated depending on the commission rates fixed by the system.
Manage Liceneces:
This page allows you to browse through issued licences and also allows you to add new licences
Customer Policy Registration:
This page all takes in all the details of the custom band maps his relationship with the agent.
